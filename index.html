CFP

Submit a talk, workshop, or performance by March 20 at 11:59 PM ET.

Steps

  1. Pick a submission type: talk, performance, or workshop.
  2. Review the entire application before starting.
  3. Fill out the application.
  4. Submit by March 20 and wait for results! You'll hear back either way.

FAQs

  • What are you looking for? Great stories from passionate coders! We're looking for submissions that inspire, educate, and are tailored to new developers.
  • Do I have to be an expert? You're an expert in your own experience, and we want to hear your story. You should be knowledgeable about what you're submitting, but you don't need expertise.
  • What if I'm submitting for the first time? We love first-timers! Our application and review process are designed to give you the best shot at being selected.
  • Can I submit a talk I've already done? Can I submit multiple times? Yes and yes! If you've already given a talk you love, submit it! And if you have multiple ideas, send us all of them!
