Opportunity Scholarship

To make this conference accessible to more people, we're offering scholarships to attend Codeland. Scholars receive a heavily discounted conference ticket at $25, as well as the potential to cover travel, depending on need and available funds.

Scholarship Application

Apply for a scholarship by Dec 25 and hear back by Jan 2. If you are part of an underrepresented group in tech, including women, members of underrepresented demographic groups*, and members of the LGBTQ community, we highly encourage you to apply.

How To Speak at Codeland

Our goal is to craft the highest quality, most engaging talks you’ve ever heard at a tech conference. We work with all our speakers to make sure your slides are engaging, your presentation is polished, and your talk is full of helpful, applicable content. Creating a talk for Codeland is a collaborative process, and we hope to work with you to bring your story to our community.

Applications due by Jan 8, 2017 at 11:59PM EST.

Apply to be a Panelist
