Talks

Talks are 15 minutes. Pick from our themes below:

  • Be Amazed
    Exciting applications for new (non-proprietary) technologies.
  • The Big Picture
    Thought-provoking stories about how code is affecting society.
  • Deep Dive
    Dives into fundamental concepts that could affect how we think about software development.

Workshops

Workshops are 2.5 hours. Pick from our topics below:

  • Creative Coding (cool, artsy things you can make with code!)
  • New tech
  • Accessibility
  • Career/Personal Branding
  • Security
  • Technical Interviews/Resume Prep
  • Computer Science Fundamentals
  • Intro to Hardware
  • Project Collaboration

Opportunity Scholarship

To make this conference accessible to more people, we're offering scholarships to attend Codeland. Scholars receive a heavily discounted conference ticket at $25, as well as assistance in covering travel expenses, depending on need and available funds.

Scholarship Application

Apply for a scholarship by May 15. If you are part of an underrepresented group in tech, including women, members of underrepresented demographic groups, and members of the LGBTQ+ community, we highly encourage you to apply.

Pay-It-Forward Ticket

Purchase a ticket at a higher rate in order to fund scholarship opportunities for other attendees. If you have the opportunity to pay a higher price, we encourage you to help out and do so.
