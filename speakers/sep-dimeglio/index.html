Gifs, the Cloud, & IoT! workshop

Get started in the cloud with Azure IoT Hub! During this workshop, participants will work together to create a website that communicates with the IoT Hub and sends a gif to the screen on an MXChip IoT Device! After completing the workshop, each participant will receive an MXChip to bring home and continue working on IoT projects independently!


Sep DiMeglio

Developer Audience Manager, Microsoft, @asksep

Sep is tasked with engaging new-to-career developers across the USA, providing them with any and all material to help ignite their next career moves. One of his most recent projects is a new podcast centered around tips & tricks for developers just starting out in the field called First5 (aka.ms/first5podcast). Sep comes from a technical background focused on web and IoT, and spends his free time designing, talking, and running UX workshops around NYC!
