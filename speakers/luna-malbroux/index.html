Laughter is the Most Serious Weapon talk

How can we use creativity, humor, and tech to solve the world's most serious issues? EquiTable creator Luna Malbroux has a few ideas. In this talk, we'll explore how meeting problems with humor can lead to real results.


Luna Malbroux

Chief Equity Officer, EquiTable

Luna Malbroux is a writer, comic, and creator of EquiTable, an app that solves the wage gap, one meal at a time. Her work has been featured in Glamour, Huffington Post, and The Atlantic to name a few, and she was recently named one of the YBCA 100, a list of the most notable cultural influencers from around the world.
