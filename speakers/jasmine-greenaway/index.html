Gifs, the Cloud, & IoT! workshop

Get started in the cloud with Azure IoT Hub! During this workshop, participants will work together to create a website that communicates with the IoT Hub and sends a gif to the screen on an MXChip IoT Device! After completing the workshop, each participant will receive an MXChip to bring home and continue working on IoT projects independently!


Jasmine Greenaway

Cloud Advocate, Microsoft, @paladique

Jasmine uses Azure to illustrate the awesome things developers can do with the cloud. Since 2009, her experience has led her to different development environments and industries. She also teaches the basics of web development as an adjunct professor at a local college. Outside of work, she volunteers as a co-organizer at BrooklynJS, a monthly meetup held in the Cobble Hill area of Brooklyn, NYC.
